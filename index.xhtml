About

The Chaos Cologne Conference brings
“hackers and painters” together to celebrate
what both cultures have in common.

We artfully break through barriers of conventions.
We have fun with things.
We build art on the edges of control.

We live in a world of chances and changes.
We are in constant need of information and inspiration
to find and create our ways through a giant maze
of borders, barriers and expectations.

We share, we steal.
We build and rebuild.
We create.

Join Us.
May 15th 'til 17th
KHM Cologne

Find your way to the maze, have fun, conquer it
and become what you are.

⚠ ⚠ Be aware! This is not an Exhibition.
This is a Challenge.
